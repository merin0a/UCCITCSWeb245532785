Maico Greg Buncio

The Flying Filipino

Greg Tecson Buncio, riding his Suzuki GSX‑R600, trains young Filipino racers in superbike techniques at Clark International Speedway part of his lifelong passion for advancing Philippine motorsports.

ACHIVEMENTS

    1996 - Won the FBO Motocross Series (50 cc category, age 10 & under) at Tagaytay

    2002 - Won the FMF Memorial Day Motocross Races (Mini Class 85 cc, age 14 & under) in Perris, California. at Tagaytay

    2007 - Became Philippine National Superbike Champion (youngest to win) by dethroning ten‑time champion Jolet Jao.

    2009 - Swept multiple wins at the Batangas Racing Circuit in the Superbikes series, broke track record (1:38.587) and won underbone classes too.

    2011 - Named “Driver of the Year” by the Golden Wheel Awards.
